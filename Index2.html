About Me

Hello my name is Madison Standing, don’t worry I don’t actually look like this. I guess my photo is a representation of how others and I see myself as an artist. I created this when I was in High School, I would say high school was when I was finding my own style. I’ve been told that my style is “strange”, “non-traditional” and “demonic”. I believe my art is strange and non-traditional but I embrace the idea, I want you the viewer to question my art and any other kind of art. I hope you embrace the strange and get a kick out of my art. Thank you for visiting my portfolio.
